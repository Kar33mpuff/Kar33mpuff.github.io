Advisors

Chis Weaver

A TSA advisor for near a decade, Mr. Weaver along with Mr. Brockman, took on Harriton TSA last year, in an effort to save the team from dissolving. Mr. Weaver coaches the study events, and programming events - events such as Chapter Team, Webmaster and Tech Bowl. His professional roles are as follows: -Welsh Valley Middle School Tech Ed teacher -Welsh Valley Middle School TSA Advisor -Harriton High School TSA Advisor (2016-present)

Tim Brockman

A TSA advisor for near a decade, Mr. Brockman along with Mr. Weaver, took on Harriton TSA last year, in an effort to save the team from dissolving. Mr. Brockman coaches many of the manufacturing and speech events in TSA, such as Dragster Design and Prepared Presentation. His professional roles are as follows: -Welsh Valley Middle School Tech Ed teacher -Welsh Valley Middle School TSA Advisor -Harriton High School TSA Advisor (2016-present)
